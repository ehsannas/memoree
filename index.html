Memoree

Master Any Subject With Flashcards and Spaced Repetition

Your Ultimate Flashcard App!

You shouldn't need to take a course to learn how to use a flashcard app! Memoree is designed to get you creating, studying, and winning in no time!

Create Decks and Sub-decks

Create decks and sub-decks to keep your flashcards in perfect order. Group topics however you like and stay on top of your studies! Easily share your decks with your friends and classmates to make studying a group effort.

Organize

Easily organize your flashcards. Got notes or flashcards in a spreadsheet? No problem! Quickly import your content into Memoree and hit the ground running.

Create Flashcards with Rich Text and Images

Add high-quality images, rich text, and even hashtags to your flashcards! Zoom in on images with a long press and make learning more visual and interactive.

Study and Review

Study your flashcards at your pace, and Memoree will remind you when you need to review cards from different topics.

Spaced Repetition

Everything works perfectly out of the box. But if you want more control, you can always modify spaced repetition settings.

Powerful Search

Looking for a specific term or hashtag? Easily search through all of your flashcards or specific decks to find what you need, when you need it!

Track Your Progress

Stay motivated by tracking your scores and progress over time. See how far you've come and what's left to conquer!
